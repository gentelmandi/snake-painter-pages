About the game

Snake Painter is a relaxing twist on the classic snake game. Every level is a blank canvas: guide your snake across the screen to paint it, and watch a unique generative artwork slowly reveal itself underneath your trail. Grow your snake by eating food, chase a completion goal each level, and unlock gallery levels that reveal real hand-picked artwork every 10 levels.

  • Endless procedurally generated levels, unique to every player
  • Relaxing, colorful painting mechanic layered on classic snake gameplay
  • Gallery levels revealing curated artwork
  • Available on iPhone and iPad
