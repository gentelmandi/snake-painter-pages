Frequently Asked Questions

My progress disappeared after reinstalling the app.
Gameplay progress is stored locally on your device only. Uninstalling the app or switching devices will reset your progress.

I'm having trouble with ads.
Ads are served through Google AdMob. If an ad fails to load, please check your internet connection and try again in a moment. If the issue persists, contact us with your device model and iOS version.

How do I manage my ad consent/privacy choices?
You can review and change your consent choices from the privacy settings link shown in the app's consent message. See our Privacy Policy for details.
